Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Introduction

Welcome to Dhaka Imperial College Humanities Club ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or become a member of our club.

If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at dic.humanitiesclub@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register as a member of DIC Humanities Club
  • Express an interest in obtaining information about us or our activities
  • Participate in activities on our website
  • Contact us via email, social media, or other communication methods

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, roll number, student ID
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number
  • Academic Information: Year of study, section, department
  • Other Information: Areas of interest, club preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our website for a variety of purposes, including:

  • To process your membership application
  • To communicate with you about club activities and events
  • To send administrative information to you
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • To manage club operations and member participation
  • To ensure compliance with college regulations

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To college authorities when required by academic regulations
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights and safety of our members and the college
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (with confidentiality agreements)

Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal information, transmission of personal information to and from our website is at your own risk.

Your Privacy Rights

As a member of DIC Humanities Club, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at dic.humanitiesclub@gmail.com.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (such as academic record-keeping requirements).

Student membership information is typically retained until graduation plus one additional academic year for alumni relations purposes.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as Facebook and Instagram). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Children's Privacy

Our services are directed to college students who are typically over 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 18, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

Last Updated: January 2024

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
